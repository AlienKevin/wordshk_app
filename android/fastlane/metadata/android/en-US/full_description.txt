Words.hk is an open Cantonese dictionary for users with various backgrounds: whether you are a Cantonese beginner or native speaker.

Features:
1. Search in English, Cantonese, or various romanizations like Jyutping and Yale
2. Handwritting recognition of Chinese characters in searches
3. Cantonese dictation in searches
4. Listen to pronunciations for words and example sentences
5. Bilingual dictionary contents in both English and Cantonese
